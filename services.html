What we offer


Our independent clinic is run by doctors and we are fully regulated and registered with the General Medical Council. We offer personalised weight loss programs specifically designed for you as an individual. We are dedicated to helping you achieve your weight loss goal and maintaining a healthy weight and lifestyle.


We aim, not only to help you reach your target weight within a realistic and healthy time frame, but also to maintain that weight with a sustainable health plan.

Our weight management program is doctor led. We require a targeted health check before setting any weight loss goals with one of our doctors.

Once you have had your initial consultation and health check, one of our doctors will discuss your options and suggest the safest and most effective weight loss plan. This may include weight loss treatments such as slimming tablets or slimming injections, as well as exercise and meal planning.

  • Free body vital tracker
  • Monthly sessions with a dietician
  • Lifetime gym membership
  • Weight management plans
  • Weekend yoga sessions
  • Weekly meal plans
